On Jealousy

Nov 2, 2025

Where does Jealousy come from? What force makes someone want to covet what others have? One way to think about jealousy might be to associate it with a desire of some form, coupled with perhaps a feeling of inadequacy at not possessing that object or entity of desire.

Could there be a positive angle to jealousy though? It would be easy to make an argument that jealousy is a cancerous emotion which keeps gnawing away steadily where it resides. It certainly is distracting by nature. Having said that, is this emotion still desirable if it leads to some personal transformation or evolution of some kind? I am not sure. I suppose jealousy will form a new object of desire once it fills the gap of what it was yearning originally.
