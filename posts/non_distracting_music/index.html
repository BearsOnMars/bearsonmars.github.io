Music which does not distract

Posted on Oct 29, 2025

Some background sound is always preferable to avoid our minds from chattering away. Well that has been my experience. I do not understand why it happens. Spiritual teachers such as the Buddha have suggested that if we are able to keep our minds from thinking about anything for even a single minute, we are already enlightened. It seems such an easy thing to say but incredibly hard to put into action.

I used to enjoy listening to rock music and reading between the lines of the songs which I love. I am perhaps someone who gets affected more than usual by what others are saying. I did not wish to be dragged in someone else’s trip anymore. What do I care about what the songwriter feels and did. I realised that I was just interested in the beats and the music itself.

Music does not need the crutch of words for it to make any sense. It is complete in itself. Here is a link to my Spotify profile with my playlists I have built over the years. So to avoid being sucked into other people’s thoughts I began to listen to pure soundtracks, classical music, jazz (a personal favorite), classical strings, hindustani classical.. and so on.

These days I just listen to Tibetan Bowl sounds. It brings me peace and actually helps me focus on the task at hand by adding a protective sound cover between myself and my undesirable thoughts.

More on spirituality and Buddha later.
