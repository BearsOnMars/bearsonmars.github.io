Tron:Ares review

Posted on Oct 28, 2025

This movie is a feast for the senses.

The red color is star of the show, which is to be expected from the title’s allusion to the Greek god of warfare and bloodshed. The cinematography is brilliant; CGI hits the sweet spot and my god the soundtrack by Nine Inch Nails! Some have even gone as far as to suggest that the movie is essentially a video companion to their music.

Highly recommended to the fans of Tron franchise. There are a lot of interesting ideas for security software designers and armchair philosophers as well.

I was particularly impressed by Jared Leto’s portrayal of the titular role, and the stoic-ness with which he plays the character whose entire being is directed towards accomplishing a mission being assigned to him. I wonder if the mythological character Ares ever wondered what lay beyond the circle of violence he was doomed to repeat. I wonder if Ares himself had a moment where he stopped to think what if he his fighting for the right cause. Zeus and Athena probably would not have liked that.

More on this mythological angle in later posts.

The soundtrack is so good and induces a sense of 80s synth nostalgia, which would find admirers in many a millenials. My favorite is the 14th track in the album Target Identified
